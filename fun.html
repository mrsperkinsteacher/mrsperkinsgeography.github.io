NASA's Landsat satellite has been photographing Earth for 50 years. Find your name written in the landscape - rivers, coastlines, deserts.

Fly anywhere on Earth in 3D. Explore the ocean floor, journey through time with historical imagery, or visit the world's wonders.

Records & Fun Facts
8,849m
Tallest mountain
Mt Everest, Nepal/Tibet - measured to the summit above sea level.
11,034m
Deepest ocean point
Challenger Deep, Mariana Trench. Deeper than Everest is tall.
6,650km
Longest river
The Nile, Africa - though the Amazon gives it a close race.
30.37M km²
Largest continent
Africa. Bigger than the USA, China, India, Europe and Japan combined.
−89.2°C
Coldest temperature ever
Vostok Station, Antarctica, July 1983.
56.7°C
Hottest temperature ever
Furnace Creek, Death Valley, California, July 1913.
25,724km
Australia's coastline
One of the longest in the world - enough to walk for years.
71%
Earth covered by ocean
And we've explored less than 20% of it. Most of our planet is still unknown.
195

193 UN member states plus Vatican City and Palestine.
