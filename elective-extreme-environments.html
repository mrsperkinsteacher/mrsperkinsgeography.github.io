️ Extreme Environments

Starting 2027 - Stage 5 Geography Elective

← Back to all elective units
Being developed Full lesson content, case studies and resources for this unit will appear here as they are built. Check back soon.
"Could you survive in the Sahara? Why do people choose to live where the Earth is trying to kill them?"
Extreme environments are places where physical conditions make human survival genuinely difficult. Understanding how these environments work - and how people and ecosystems adapt - is one of the most compelling areas of physical geography.
