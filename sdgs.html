United Nations

Sustainable Development Goals

In 2015, every country agreed on 17 goals for a better, fairer world by 2030. Click any goal to go directly to its UN page.

UN SDG Action Campaign

Going deeper on the Goals

The UN SDG Action Campaign produces resources, videos and campaign materials to help people engage with the Goals. The links below are organised by format so you can find the right resource for your purpose.

Watch and learn

Video libraries and channels with expert interviews, explainers and campaign content directly from the UN.

SDG Action Campaign Virtual Library
Website

A curated video library from the UN SDG Action Campaign. Organised by theme including economic justice, gender equality and climate action. Includes short expert interviews and SDG soundbites suitable for classroom use.

Visit Virtual Library →
SDG Action Campaign YouTube Channel
YouTube

The official YouTube channel of the UN SDG Action Campaign. Features campaign videos, award winner profiles and interviews with advocates from around the world. Good for finding short, shareable content on specific Goals.

Visit Channel →
United Nations DESA YouTube Channel
YouTube

The UN Department of Economic and Social Affairs publishes presentations, high-level forum recordings and progress briefings here. Useful for more detailed policy-level content and official SDG reporting events.

Visit Channel →

Take action and get involved

Campaign boards run by the UN SDG Action Campaign with ideas, resources and calls to action around the Goals. Open to anyone who wants to get involved.

Flip the Script Campaign Board
Trello

A campaign action board from the UN SDG Action Campaign. Organised around storytelling and changing the narrative on sustainable development. Useful for finding creative ways to communicate the Goals.

Open Board →
Unite to Act Campaign Board
Trello

A campaign coordination board from the UN SDG Action Campaign with resources and actions for mobilising people around the Goals. Good for seeing how global campaigns are organised and communicated.

Open Board →
SDG Butterfly Effect Campaign Board
Trello

Explores the idea that individual and community actions create ripple effects across the Goals. A useful entry point for discussing interconnection between the SDGs and the role everyday choices play in sustainable development.

Open Board →
