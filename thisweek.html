This Week in Geography

Current news stories - and what they mean for the world we study.

Update this week's stories

Edit the JSON below - one story per block. Replace the text between the quote marks. Keep the commas and brackets exactly as they are. When done, copy the whole thing, go to your GitHub repo, open thisweek.json, paste it in and commit.

Each story needs these four things:

"title" - the headline
"url" - the link to the article
"source" - where it's from (ABC, BBC, Guardian etc)
"date" - the week (e.g. "Week of 26 May 2026")
"connection" - your one or two sentence geography note
"sdg" - the SDG it links to

After copying - go to GitHub, open thisweek.json, select all, paste, commit. Your changes will be live within 30 seconds.
