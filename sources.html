Geography Sources

Reliable, free sources for current geography news, research and case studies - all clickable, no paywall.

Every source on this page is free to read and student-friendly. Use these when you need a current example for an assessment, want to find a case study nobody else will use, or just want to understand what's happening in the world through a geography lens. Mrs Perkins uses these to find stories for This Week in Geography.
How to find a good case study
Search the source's website for your topic - e.g. "coral bleaching" or "food security Australia" or "South China Sea." Look for articles from the last 2-3 years. The best case studies are specific - a named place, a real event, with data. Avoid articles that are just opinion with no evidence.
